## Purpose

Full e-commerce platform for an independent clothing brand. Catalogue, cart, checkout and an admin dashboard in a single product.

## Technical decisions

- **Next.js App Router** for SSR of the catalogue and SEO-friendly per-product routes.
- **Stripe Checkout** in hosted mode to stay out of PCI scope; webhooks to confirm orders.
- **PostgreSQL** with Prisma; the catalogue is served from an ISR cache and invalidated on-demand when stock changes.

## Challenges

Keeping inventory in sync between the admin dashboard and Stripe without race conditions. Solved with serializable transactions and an optimistic lock per SKU.
